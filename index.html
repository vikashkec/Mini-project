About Us

The Indian fitness industry is undergoing a revolution of sorts spiked by the increasing incidences of obesity and diabetes. If we look around, we will find that weight loss advertisements are rampant everywhere. Spending on fitness was earlier seen as a luxury, but now it is a way of life. Not just in the urban areas and cities, but also in tier 2 and tier 3 cities and towns and even in the villages, where people are increasingly opting for wellness and fitness.

A preventive approach to healthcare has led to an increase in demand for not just services but also products. Today is health conscious and fitness aware generation wants to look and feel good at any cost and this is further fuelling the growth of the fitness resources and services industry on the whole. Our Service

Crossfit Training

CrossFit is a strength and conditioning program that uses constantly varying functional movements executed at high intensity. We aim at improving stamina, strength, flexibility, power, speed, balance, and accuracy.

Cadio Training

Our wide range of treadmills, bikes, and cross-trainers gives a facility the freedom to choose fitness equipment that fits its needs and improving cardiovascular endurance. Burn fat fast with our international trained trainers and equipment.

Strength Training

Use our resistance machines and free weights to induce muscular contraction which builds the strength, and size of skeletal muscles. Our plate loaded machines from Impulse Sterling are designed to replicate the feeling of using free weights.
